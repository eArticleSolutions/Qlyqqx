The Four Variables

Your brew is a conversation between four variables. Your recipe mentions one.

"Ferment 7–10 days" was written for 24°C, a vigorous mother, 7% sugar tea, average humidity. Your shelf is 20°C. Your SCOBY is on its eighth generation. Each variable shifts the window by days — compounding, silently.

Ambient Temp
±4d
A 4°C swing reshapes your whole fermentation window.
SCOBY Vigor
±3d
A tired mother on gen 12 ≠ a fresh one. The math is different.
Sugar · Tea
±2d
6 vs 8% sugar changes acid build and bottling timing.
Humidity
±1d
The variable nobody writes about — and it stacks.
